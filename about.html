Our Story

J&M Electrical Service was founded on a straightforward belief: every customer — whether a homeowner or a business owner — deserves exceptional electrical work at a price they can actually afford.

With more than 35 years of hands-on experience under our belt, we've tackled just about every electrical challenge a home or business can throw at us. From simple outlet installations to complete commercial buildouts and 3-phase service installations, we bring the same level of professionalism and care to every job.

As a family-owned business established right here in San Antonio, we're locally rooted and community-focused. We don't just do a job and move on — we build lasting relationships. Our customers come back to us again and again across multiple properties because they know what they're going to get: honest assessments, fair pricing, and quality work done right.

Whether it's same-day service for an urgent repair or a carefully planned commercial project, you'll always get the same dedicated team that treats your home or business with the respect it deserves.
